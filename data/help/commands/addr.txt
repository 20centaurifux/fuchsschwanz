USAGE
     /addr {street address}
     /addr

DESCRIPTION
     Sets the street address of the registered user. You can use a pipe as
     delimiter.

     Without an argument the street address is unset.

EXAMPLE
     /addr Jeremy Martinson, Jr. | 455 Larkspur Dr. Apt 23 | Baviera, CA 92908

SEE ALSO
     registration /rname /phone /email /text /www /whois
