USAGE
     /email {email address}
     /email

DESCRIPTION
     Sets the email address of the registered user.

     Without an argument the email address is unset.

     Confirm your email address to enable message forwarding or resetting
     your password.

SEE ALSO

     /confirm /forward /noforward inbox /protect /noprotect
